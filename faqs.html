What is SPLITO?

SPLITO is a new age credit card that gives you flexibility to split your monthly credit card bill into 3 EMIs at zero interest.

Key benefits of SPLITO:

  • Split bills into 3 EMIs at Zero interest charges
  • Option to Split bills into 6,9 or 12 EMI’s at attractive interest rates
  • Get SPLITO Card instantly through end-to-end digital onboarding journey
  • No annual or joining fees
  • Exciting reward program & host of offers that help you save on your spends

How can I get SPLITO Card?

SPLITO Card is issued instantly through end-to-end digital onboarding journey. It’s a fast & paperless process. Simply follow the below steps:

Step 1: Download the SPLITO App from App Store or Play Store

Step 2: Sign up by verifying your mobile number and Email id

Step 3: Complete your KYC online

Step 4: Activate your card and you’re all set

What do ‘SplitPay in 3 EMIs’, ‘SplitPay in 6 EMIs’, ‘SplitPay in 9 EMIs’ and ‘SplitPay in 12 EMIs’ mean?

You can pay your SPLITO bill either in Full or in EMIs. You can also use a combination of ‘Pay in Full’ and SplitPay EMIs.

SplitPay EMI options:

  • ‘SplitPay in 3 EMIs’ : Split your credit card bill into 3 EMIs, interest free
  • ‘SplitPay in 6 EMIs : Split your credit card bill into 6 EMIs. Similarly, you may also opt for 9 or 12 EMI options.

What are the fees and charges on SPLITO Card?

SPLITO is a free card with no joining or annual fee. There are no hidden/service charges that you need to worry about.

However, you will be required to pay interest charges if you split your bill payment in 6,9 or 12 EMI’s. Also, late fee charges are applicable on any missed payments by the due date. For more details on fees and charges, please refer to the MITC

What are the interest rates applicable for ‘SplitPay EMI in 3, 6, 9 and 12’ options?

’SplitPay in 3 EMIs’ is available to you at Zero interest rate ie. NO interest will be charged on the principal amount.

The applicable interest amount on 6,9 or 12 EMI option will be shown to you when you select any of these EMI options on the app.

To know more details applicable interest rates for 6,9 & 12 SplitPay EMI options, pls refer toMITC

How does the contactless technology on SPLITO Card work?

Step 1: Look for the Visa contactless technology mark and contactless logo at the point of sale.
Step 2: The cashier enters your purchase amount into the terminal. This amount will be displayed on the contactless reader.
Step 3: Hold your card over the reader at close range (less than 4 cm from where the contactless logo appears).
Step 4: Four green indicator lights or a beep sound will indicate when the transaction is complete. A message will also be displayed on the screen indicating the transaction is complete. No PIN is required.

Please note that payment through contactless mode is allowed for a maximum of ₹5000 for a single transaction.

What are the different types of transactions where I can use this card?

You can use your SPLITO for a variety of transactions – be it online/offline or domestic/international. Using the SPLITO mobile app, you can customize your transaction limits for contactless, online &amp; Point of Sale (POS) transactions.

Is there a limit on number of transactions on SPLITO?

There is no limit on the number of transactions that you can make with SLPITO. However, if your credit limit is exhausted, you will not be able to make any further transactions until the bill is paid.

What are the different EMI plans that SPLITO offers?

SPLITO gives you the flexibility to split your monthly credit card bill into SplitPay EMIs. You can choose to Pay in Full or in EMIs or using a combination of both

Do I get any reward points? How do I earn these rewards?

With SPLITO, you earn reward points every time you pay your SPLITO bill in full on or before the bill due date. These reward points are called SPLITO Credits.
 
SPLITO Credits will not be applicable for SplitPay EMI options. However, if you choose to split your bill amount into Pay in Full and EMIs, SPLITO Credits will be given for amount that is paid in full.

‍
For every Re. 1 paid in full, you get 1 SPLITO Credit. The value of SPLITO Credit is 0.5% and it can be redeemed against any of your subsequent card bill(s).

‍

A.
Bill Paid in Full – Rs. 10,000
SPLITO Credits earned – 10,000
Value of SPLITO Credits – Rs. 50

‍

B.‍

Bill Paid in Full – Rs. 7,000

EMI taken – Rs 3,000

SPLITO Credits earned – 7,000

Value of SPLITO Credits – Rs 35 (applicable on Rs 7,000)

Value of SPLITO Credits can be adjusted against any of your subsequent card bill(s)
